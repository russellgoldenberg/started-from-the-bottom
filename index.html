Started from the bottom

By Russell Goldenberg

It has been 39 years since the ABA-NBA merger of 1976. In that time, every team (without exception) has descended to the depths of the rankings. Here is a closer look at the various paths NBA franchises have taken to get from the bottom to the top of league.

Let’s start by looking at end-of-season rankings based on games won across the league. We will consider a team hitting bottom or top if they have of one of the four worst or best records, respectively.

The Golden State Warriors finished atop the rankings for the second consecutive year, setting the record with 73 wins this season.
