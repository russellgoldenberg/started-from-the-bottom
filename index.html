Started from the bottom

By Russell Goldenberg

It has been 39 years since the ABA-NBA merger of 1976. In that time, every team (without exception) has descended to the depths of the rankings. Here is a closer look at the various paths NBA franchises have taken to get from the bottom to the top of league.

Let’s start by looking at end-of-season rankings based on games won across the league. We will consider a team reaching the top or hitting rock bottom if they have of one of the four best or worst records, respectively.

The Golden State Warriors finished the 2015-16 season atop the rankings for the second consecutive year, passing the Chicago Bulls record with 73 wins.
